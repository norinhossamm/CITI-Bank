ABOUT US

We make it easier for you!

Citibank, also known as Citi, is a global financial institution that provides a wide range of financial products and services to individuals, businesses, and governments. Founded in 1812 as the City Bank of New York, Citi has a long history of innovation and has played a significant role in the development of modern banking.

Today, Citi operates in more than 160 countries and jurisdictions, serving over 200 million customer accounts and employing over 200,000 people worldwide. The bank offers a variety of services, including banking, credit cards, loans, mortgages, investment products, and wealth management. Citi's banking services include checking and savings accounts, as well as online and mobile banking options. The bank also offers credit cards with a range of rewards and benefits, including cash back, points, and miles. For those seeking loans, Citi provides personal loans, student loans, and home loans, as well as small business loans and lines of credit. The bank's mortgage services include refinancing, home equity loans, and reverse mortgages.
